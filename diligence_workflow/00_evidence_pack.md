## Evidence Gate

Before moving to agent review, answer:

- Can every major fact be traced to a source?
- Are source dates current enough for the decision?
- Are there any facts that are likely to change quickly, such as prices, yields, ratings, spreads, or guidance?
- Which unsupported assumption is currently the weakest link?

Core rule:

> No final thesis can be stronger than the weakest unsupported assumption.
